Token

At the first stage, GON will be an ERC20 token on the Ethereum blockchain. After the Mainnet is released, GON blockchain will be integrated into our partner’s platform and the GON ERC20 tokens will be swapped to GON Mainnet coins.

GONs will be built as a fixed-supply cryptocurrency. The total supply of GON will be 100,000,000,000 GON. The initial distribution will be 30,000,000,000 GON.

Other 70,000,000,000 GON will be distributed within 7 years. Each quarter, 2,500,000,000 GON will be distributed.

15% Research and Development

Compensation for the parties involved in the Research and Development of GON ecosystem, this budget will be allocated strategically within 7 years, after that, a part of our partners' profits will be dedicated to the GON Foundation for R&D activities.

55% Strategic Partners Purchase

To integrate with our GON platform, our partners must purchase a large amount of GONs for GONs lock-up to maintain the stability of GON blockchain as well as distribution to their customers to use in the ecosystem.
